  • QUATERLOO


    Stack Exchange for UW students

    The perks

  • Exclusive

    Quaterloo is a network exclusive to uWaterloo students - you need an official UW id, which gets authenticated against CAS, to get in. While most other Q&A platforms are open to the public, we've decided to keep this one internal to the UW community.

  • Anonymous

    Quaterloo is built to be fully anonymous. While you are required to authenticate with your UW credentials, we don't actually store any of your info apart from your user id, and absolutely no user information is used anywhere on the site. Unlike other networks that stress identity, you can ask and answer questions anonymously in the UW community.

  • Freedom

    Ask whatever you want, and rest assured knowing that your questions will be both anonymous and visible only to other UW students.
